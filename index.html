Introduction

This Project aims at visualizing the population of the 25 most densely populated cities in India currently, in the years !971, 2001 and 2011, and using this information to analyse changes in the population density.

New Delhi and Ghaziabad

The population of New Delhi increases rapidly between 1971 to 2001. From 2001 to 2011, the increase in population becomes very slow and almost negligible. An interesting corelation and a possible effect of this can be seen on the neighbouring Ghaziabad whose population increases rapidly from 2001 to 2011.

Mumbai and Thane

The population of Mumbai increases rapidly between 1971 to 2001. From 2001 to 2011, the increase in population is almost almost negligible. A similar interesting corelation and a possible effect of this can be seen on the neighbouring Thane whose population increase is much more substantial from 2001 to 2011.
